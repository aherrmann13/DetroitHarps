Our Mission

We are a volunteer-run organization dedicated to the promotion and preservation of youth Gaelic games in the metro Detroit area.

The Detroit Harps Gaelic Football Club

Our summer Gaelic Football program is open to boys and girls ages 5-16. We meet on Sundays evenings in the summer at Flodin Park in Canton, MI. Please see our Schedule link for additional details. New players are always welcome. Experience with Gaelic Football is not required. Through the generosity of our sponsors, club supporters and volunteers, we are able to keep the cost of our program to a nominal fee. Our goal is to encourage as many kids as possible to try out this exciting sport. For any question or further information, please see our Contact page.
