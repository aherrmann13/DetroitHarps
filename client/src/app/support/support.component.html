Please Support Us

Our primary purpose is the promotion and development of youth Gaelic games in our area. Our programs are run entirely by volunteers. We are 501(c)(3) non-profit organization. Please consider supporting us in one of the following areas


Sponsorship

While our programs are expanding considerably this year, we intend to keep the registration fees for the kids to a minimum. This is to make our programs accessible to as many kids as possible and to encourage new kids to try out Gaelic football. If you are aware of any businesses or organizations that may be interested in sponsorship, please ask them to contact us at detroitharps@gmail.com

Donations

Tax deductible donations can be made on our website through Paypal or mailed to:
28156 North Clement Circle
Livonia 48150
(please make checks out to Detroit Youth GAA)

Volunteer

We are always in need of volunteers. Please consider volunteering. We need help with various aspects of the administration of the club and with coaching and referees for our summer league. There is no experience with Gaelic football required to help with coaching. Also we will be hosting a free 1-day foundation coaching course in the spring of 2016 for all coaches in our club. More details will follow.
